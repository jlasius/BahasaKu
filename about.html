About BahasaKu

Explore the richness of Malay idioms in a fun and accessible way.

Hi, I’m Julian, the creator of BahasaKu! While studying Bahasa Malaysia and interning at Dewan Bahasa dan Pustaka under the guidance of some exceptional mentors, I became fascinated by Malay idioms - expressions that offer cultural insights beyond direct translation. I wanted to create something fun and easy to use while also experimenting with AI in the process.

BahasaKu lets you browse insights into Malaysia and quiz yourself on idioms anytime. Meet Sang, our clever mousedeer mascot, inspired by those who use idioms masterfully. If I find the time, I’ll create more projects, like a guide to mastering Bahasa’s tricky imbuhan grammar forms. Stay tuned!
